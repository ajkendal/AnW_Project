Electric Scales

Auto 44

With a capacity of 44 lbs (20 kg) and readability of .5oz (10g), the Auto 44 scale will accurately weigh small animals. The scale can operate on AC power (adapter included) or a single 9 volt DC battery. Hold button on the console allows you to lock in an electronically-averaged weight for restless animals to keep the reading from “jumping around.”
